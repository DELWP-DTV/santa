Data Catalogue Uptime Status

The status page for the Digital Twin Victoria Platform Catalogue. You can use SANTA to automate the process of checking web service endpoints in a catalog. It can analyze the endpoints, test their functionality, and provide valuable insights and reports. SANTA can be a valuable tool for developers and quality assurance teams to ensure the smooth operation of web services in a catalog. Data assets are checked every 12 hours.
